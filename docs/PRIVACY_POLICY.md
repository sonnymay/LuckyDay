**Effective date:** May 1, 2026
**Last updated:** May 1, 2026

LuckyDay ("we," "our," or "the app") is operated by [YOUR NAME / COMPANY NAME] ("I," "me"). This Privacy Policy explains what information LuckyDay collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

---

## 1. Summary (Plain Language)

- **LuckyDay stores all your data on your device only.** We do not run servers that receive or store your personal information.
- **We do not sell your data.** Ever.
- **We do not share your data** with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties.
- **Photos you add are stored only on your device** and are never transmitted anywhere.
- **Subscriptions are handled by Apple.** We never see your payment details.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2a. Information you provide directly

When you create a LuckyDay profile, you optionally provide:

| Data | Purpose | Stored where |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Nickname | Personalize your daily greeting | On your device only (AsyncStorage) |
| Birthday | Calculate Chinese zodiac, lucky number, personalized reading | On your device only |
| Birth time | Optional — future reading depth | On your device only |
| Birthplace | Optional — future reading depth | On your device only |
| Main focus areas | Personalize daily reading content | On your device only |
| Notification time | Schedule daily local reminders | On your device only |
| Face photo | Optional — for future reading features | On your device only |
| Palm photos (left/right) | Optional — for future reading features | On your device only |
| Handwriting photo | Optional — for future reading features | On your device only |

**None of the above is transmitted to any server.** It is stored using Apple's AsyncStorage API on your personal device.

### 2b. Information we do NOT collect

- We do not collect your email address.
- We do not collect precise location data.
- We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) directly.
- We do not run analytics that track your in-app behavior.
- We do not collect crash logs (no crash reporting SDK is installed).

### 2c. Subscription information (via Apple / RevenueCat)

If you subscribe to LuckyDay Premium, the transaction is processed by Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) to verify your subscription status.

- **RevenueCat receives:** A randomly-generated anonymous identifier provided by Apple, your subscription status, and purchase timestamps.
- **RevenueCat does NOT receive:** Your name, email, payment details, or any personal profile data from LuckyDay.
- RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

We never receive or store your credit card or payment information.

---

## 3. Photos

If you choose to add photos (face, palms, handwriting), those photos:

- Are stored only on your device using iOS local file storage.
- Are **never uploaded** to any server, cloud, or third-party service.
- Are accessed only by the LuckyDay app to display them within the app.
- Can be deleted at any time via **Settings → Delete photos only** or **Settings → Delete all local data**.

**Important:** These photos may be sensitive biometric information. We take this seriously. They remain entirely in your control, on your device, at all times.

---

## 4. Local Notifications

If you set a daily reminder time, LuckyDay schedules a local notification on your device. This notification is generated and delivered entirely on-device by iOS. No notification data is sent to any server.

You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings → LuckyDay → Notifications.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device:

- **You control all data.** You can delete it at any time.
- To delete your profile and all reading history: **Settings → Delete all local data**.
- To delete photos only: **Settings → Delete photos only**.
- To delete feedback ratings: **Settings → Clear feedback**.
- Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored LuckyDay data from your device.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

LuckyDay is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you. (All data is on your device — you already have full access.)
- **Delete** your personal data. (Use the in-app delete controls in Settings, or uninstall the app.)
- **Correct** your personal data. (Use Settings to update your profile.)
- **Data portability.** Because your data never leaves your device, there is no transfer to request.

For California residents (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

For EU/UK residents (GDPR): Our legal basis for processing your locally-stored data is your consent, given when you create your profile and accept this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your profile.

## 9. Security

Because LuckyDay stores data locally using iOS AsyncStorage:

- Your data is protected by your device's built-in encryption (when the device is locked with a passcode or biometrics).
- AsyncStorage is not independently encrypted at the application layer — we recommend using a device passcode.
- We do not transmit your data, so there is no transmission interception risk.
