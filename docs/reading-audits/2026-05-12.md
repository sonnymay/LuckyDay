## Themes for next rotation

1. **Time-of-day anchors** — every reading should tell user *when* to act, not just *what*
2. **Concrete verbs** — "Price", "Name", "Lay out" beats abstract "Focus on", "Trust", "Believe"
3. **Five-element grounding** — Wood/Fire/Earth/Metal/Water references reinforce the Chinese Almanac positioning over generic horoscope language
4. **User as agent, not recipient** — replace "Abundance finds you" with "Notice what's already feeding you"
5. **Sensory imagery over abstraction** — stones, water, sleeves rolled up beat "approach", "context", "matters"
