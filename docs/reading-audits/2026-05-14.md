**Pool:** `src/lib/almanac.ts`
**Rubric:** HOPE / SPECIFIC / USEFUL / ALMANAC VOICE / FUN

## Sample pass

Ran `e2e/sample-readings.ts` for 10 varied fake profiles on 2026-05-14. The daily feed repeatedly surfaced:

- `goodFor`: "New connections · Love & relationships · Ritual & prayer"
- `avoid`: "Large financial decisions · Making big agreements"

The almanac data was accurate, but the weakest strings read like category tags. For a user, "New connections" and "Ritual & prayer" do not say what to do, and "Large financial decisions" sounds corporate instead of grounded.

---

## Selected for rewrite (3 lowest-scoring)

### 1. `src/lib/almanac.ts:25`

**Before:**
> "Ritual & prayer"

**Score (1-5):** Hope 3 · Specific 2 · Useful 1 · Almanac voice 3 · Fun 1

**Issues:** Accurate but stiff. It gestures toward tradition without giving the user a small, non-cringe action.

**After:**
> "Light a candle, give thanks"

**Why better:**
- Concrete ritual: light + gratitude
- Softly almanac-coded without sounding mystical-heavy
- Works as a quick "Good for today" pill

---

### 2. `src/lib/almanac.ts:29`

**Before:**
> "New connections"

**Score (1-5):** Hope 3 · Specific 1 · Useful 1 · Almanac voice 1 · Fun 1

**Issues:** Generic app taxonomy. No verb, no warmth, no user agency.

**After:**
> "Offer a warm first hello"

**Why better:**
- Imperative plus permission: user can act without forcing intimacy
- Warmer and more human than a category label
- Keeps the "new connection" meaning while making it do something

---

### 3. `src/lib/almanac.ts:65`

**Before:**
> "Large financial decisions"

**Score (1-5):** Hope 2 · Specific 2 · Useful 2 · Almanac voice 1 · Fun 1

**Issues:** Sounds like a bank form. It warns, but without a plain human instruction.

**After:**
> "Pause big money moves"

**Why better:**
- Clear verb: pause
- Friendlier than "financial decisions"
- Still compact enough for an "Avoid today" pill

## Verification sample after rewrite

## Themes for next rotation

1. **Keep labels action-shaped** — even short pills should tell the user what to do.
2. **Avoid corporate nouns** — "money moves" beats "financial decisions" in this product voice.
3. **Make tradition tactile** — candles, thanks, first hellos, tea, paper, doors, and clean surfaces beat abstract spirituality.
4. **Audit remaining avoid labels** — "Making big agreements" is still serviceable but wants a warmer, more visual pass later.
