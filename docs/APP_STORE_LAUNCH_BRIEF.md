# LuckyDay App Store Launch Brief

Last updated: 2026-04-29

## Positioning

LuckyDay is a private daily luck ritual for women who want a gentle, beautiful way to check the energy of the day before they move through it.

Core promise:

> Every morning, LuckyDay gives you a personal luck energy reading: your lucky color, number, best time, moon energy, Thai day color, and one small action to help you move with intention.

Keep the app away from generic horoscope positioning. The niche is daily luck, color ritual, Asian/Thai-inspired timing, manifestation, and private self-care.

## App Store Subtitle Options

Apple subtitle limit is 30 characters.

- Daily luck & color ritual
- Daily luck and moon guide
- Lucky color, number, timing
- Your morning luck ritual
- Daily fortune and lucky color

Recommended first test:

> Daily luck & color ritual

## Keywords

Use a focused keyword set rather than stuffing every spiritual term.

Primary:

`luck, lucky color, daily luck, fortune, daily reading, manifestation, moon phase, lucky number, Thai horoscope`

Secondary:

`zodiac, self care, ritual, feng shui, astrology, palm reading, horoscope, numerology, morning routine`

## Short Description

LuckyDay is your private morning luck ritual. Check today’s luck energy, lucky color, number, best time, moon energy, and Thai day color before you start your day.

## App Description Draft

Every morning has its own energy.

LuckyDay helps you start the day with a simple private ritual: open the app, reveal your luck energy, and see the color, number, time, direction, moon phase, and Thai day color that can guide your day.

Use LuckyDay to:

- Reveal your daily luck energy score
- Find your lucky color, number, time, and direction
- See today’s moon energy and Thai day color
- Choose a small action for better luck
- Save and share a beautiful 9:16 daily luck card
- Track your daily ritual streak and reading history
- Keep your profile and optional photos local to your device

LuckyDay is designed for gentle daily guidance, not fear-based predictions. Your profile stays private by default, and optional face, palm, and handwriting photos stay on your device in this version.

## Screenshot Capture Rules

- Use a real iPhone-sized viewport or device.
- Prefer a profile with a short friendly nickname like `Mali`.
- Use a strong-looking score: 74-88 if possible.
- Avoid screenshots where the lucky color is black or white for the first App Store pass.
- Do not include optional personal photos in screenshots.
- Do not show permission dialogs in screenshots.
- Keep overlay copy short: 5-7 words.
- Make sure all text fits at small App Store preview sizes.

## Monetization Direction

Launch v1 should keep the core daily ritual free.

Free:

- Daily luck energy
- Lucky color, number, time, direction
- Moon energy
- Thai day color
- One share card template
- Reading history and streak

Premium later:

- Multiple share-card themes
- Animated share cards
- Weekly/monthly lucky outlook
- Deeper photo-based readings
- Personalized notification copy
- Expanded history insights

Do not charge for the first share-card template. It is the growth loop.

## Launch Blockers

Do not submit to App Store until these are verified on real devices:

- Camera capture works for face, palms, and handwriting.
- Share card saves sharply to Photos.
- Share sheet hands off cleanly to Instagram, LINE, WhatsApp, and Messages.
- Notification permission, scheduling, changing, and disabling work.
- Optional photos persist after app restart.
- Delete all local data clears profile, photos, feedback, history, and reminders.
- Small iPhone screens do not clip onboarding or share-card text.
